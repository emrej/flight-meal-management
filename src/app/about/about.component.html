Flight Meal Management Github Repo

 

Fill in the flight number and flight departure date then click 'Add flight'

Flights will be listed below the form.

After adding the flight you can add economy and business class meals, fill in number of meals and click 'Add meal'

Meals also will be listed with the flights, if you click on a flight, flight info will be auto filled into input boxes.

Flights may be deleted via filling the details in the input fields or selecting the flight from the list, then clicking 'Delete flight'
